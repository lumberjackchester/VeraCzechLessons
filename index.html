Lessons

Let's learn Czech.

All my life I've worked with the Czech language, first as an editor for printed media, and later as a Czech teacher. Czech is, in my opinion, the richest language in the world, but also one of the most difficult. In order to master Czech, you need strong motivation and a lot of pa-tience. The students that I teach has both, although for others it is also an interesting hobby. I use exclusively Czech to teach during my lessons, using different ways of describing things, humour, gestures, photos and illustrations to help my students understand better. I only use English when I'm trying to explain Czech grammar.

I respect my students’ needs and I design the lessons to be the most effective and practical way to achieve their goals. Location: Prague and surrounding areas (max. 30 km commute). Our lessons can be held in your office, your home, over SKYPE or we can meet in a quiet café in the center of Prague. Contact me for more information
